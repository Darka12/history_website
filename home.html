Introduction

This website was created for the purpose of exploring the history of the City of Plymouth during World War 2. In particular exploring the years 1940 to 1944 which is when Plymouth was going through a series of German air raids known as the Plymouth Blitz.

Accessibility

Below are various accessibility features that have been implemented with the purpose to make this website inclusive and accessible to all people:

  • Images with alternative text (used by screen readers)
  • Large font sizes
  • Contrasting colours
  • Videos
  • Screen reader options where possible
